Patents & Know-How

Co-inventor of multiple patents in super-resolution and time-resolved optical microscopy, with technologies licensed and used in commercial imaging systems.

  • TIME-RESOLVED IMAGING METHOD WITH HIGH SPATIAL RESOLUTION A method for operating high-resolution time-resolved optical imaging systems, widely adopted in advanced microscopy. International application WO2019/145889 A1 (patent family), with granted patents in key markets including the United States (US 11,422,348 B2), Europe (EP 3743758 B1), Japan and China. Licensed to Nikon and implemented in the NSPARC commercial microscope module. https://www.microscope.healthcare.nikon.com/en_EU/products/confocal-microscopes/ax

  • SIMULTANEOUS MULTI-SPECIES SUPER-RESOLUTION IMAGING VIA TEMPORAL MULTIPLEXING AND SINGLE- PHOTON DETECTOR ARRAY A method for operating high-resolution time-resolved optical imaging systems, widely adopted in advanced microscopy. International application WO2023/275777A1 A1 (patent family), with granted patents.

  • STED Super-Resolution Microscopy Technology Know-How related to STED fluorescence microscope design and implementation, licensed to the ISS microscopy company. https://iss.com/fluorescence/time-resolved-laser-scanning-confocal-microscopes/alba-sted
